About BrickBaltic LUG

BrickBaltic LUG is the largest LEGO User Group in the Baltic states, welcoming builders of all ages and skill levels since 2012. We meet monthly in Riga and host three major public exhibitions per year, including the annual BrickFest Baltic which draws 2,000+ visitors.

Our members specialize in everything from large-scale city layouts and Technic builds to intricate castle MOCs and Space dioramas.
